user experience and service design (msc)mOdvision

This project aimed to create a smart glasses system based on AR technology to assist people with their every day activities. ModVision is able to enhance people's senses by blending the real and the virtual world through AR. That is the reason my team and I believe that ModVision would be a great tool for hearing or vision impaired people. For this project, I created a mobile application prototype on Axure that would be able to connect, control and customise the glasses. For the research part, we analysed features offered by similar systems while we also reached out to prospective users using online platforms. For the testing and evaluation of the project we used UX experts, the Wizard of Oz methos and a SUS questionaire. The Axure project can be found here: https://2igrq3.axshare.com/#id=9k4p77

design dialogues (msc)perma-garden

The aim of this project was to design a system to help people become more effective permaculture agriculturists. The whole project was based on the Scenario-based method. In order to understand more about permaculture and the people using it (or willing to use it) a research was conducted both online (60% of participants) through social media and with face to face interviews (40% of participants). The tools used were questionnaires and open questions. Using the data from our research, User-Stories and Personas were developed that lead to Conceptual and Concrete Scenarios. Finaly, a list of functional and non-functional requirments was produced along with sketches and a design language. The mockaps were crated in Adobe XD.
